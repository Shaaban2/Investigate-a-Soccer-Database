Research Question 4 (Is there a relationship between win percentage and Pythagorean expectation?)¶

win percentage(wpc) calculate the percentage of wins of games(wins value/ Games played) pythagorean takes my goals in the opponent and the goals of the opponent against me it has a formula and it can predicts the performance of the teams in the future

In [140]:
EPL['W'] = EPL['hwinvalue'].values + EPL['awinvalue'].values
EPL['Games_played'] = EPL['matches_home'].values +EPL ['matchesAway'].values
